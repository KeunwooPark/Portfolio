Keunwoo Park

I build Serok, a platform where users can log thoughts that pop up while reading books.

I was the chief product officer at Ordinary Magic Corp., a company based in South Korea, making toys for infants and toddlers under the Peekaby brand. I led the hardware toy team and the software team.


I completed my Ph.D. at KAIST HCIL. During my Ph.D., I focused on technologies for physical human-computer interaction. I developed technologies to 1) bring the existing interaction concepts closer to our life, and 2) support novel interaction ideas. My projects include smartwatch interactions, haptic devices, fabrication, and hand sensing technologies.


Prior to my Ph.D., I completed a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science at KAIST. I also completed a master's degree in Computer Science at KAIST.
